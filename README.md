## What We Offer

BagBuddy provides secure, tech-enabled luggage logistics designed to make urban travel seamless and unburdened. Our services include:

### 1. Self-Service Storage

On-demand storage at verified BagBuddy partner locations (cafes, shops, etc.).
Travelers drop off and pick up their bags themselves using a QR-based digital check-in.

### 2. Door-to-Door Delivery

Flexible, non-airport luggage transfers between any two city points.
Examples:

* Drop off at a partner shop, we deliver to your accommodation
* We collect from your hotel, you pick up later at a partner location

### 3. Airport Delivery (Luggage Only)

Travelers drop off their bags at a downtown partner location, and BagBuddy delivers them to the airport for collection and check-in.

### 4. Airport Transfer (Passenger + Luggage)

A bundled solution combining:

* Luggage drop-off at a city location
* Secure luggage delivery to the airport
* Passenger transport to the airport
  Ideal for a fully stress-free departure.

### 5. MICE & Event Logistics

Full-scale luggage management for conferences, expos, concerts, and group events.
Includes:

* Branded, staffed on-site storage booths
* Shuttle-style luggage delivery to hotels or the airport
  Event organizers can earn 25% commission on stored bags.
